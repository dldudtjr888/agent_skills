## 필름/후처리 효과

### 필름 시뮬레이션
- Kodak Portra 400 (따뜻한 피부톤, 부드러운 색감)
- Kodak Ektar 100 (선명한 색감, 높은 대비)
- Fujifilm Velvia (강한 채도, 풍경에 적합)
- Fujifilm Pro 400H (부드러운 파스텔톤)
- Ilford HP5 (클래식 흑백)
- CineStill 800T (텅스텐 조명, 할레이션)

### 색감 처리
- cinematic color grading (시네마틱 색보정)
- teal and orange grading (할리우드 색감)
- desaturated, muted colors (차분한 저채도)
- vibrant, saturated colors (선명한 고채도)
- monochrome, black and white (흑백)
- cross-processed (크로스 프로세싱)
- warm earth tones (따뜻한 어스톤)
- cool blue tones (차가운 블루톤)

### 질감/효과
- film grain (필름 그레인)
- shallow depth of field, bokeh (얕은 피사계 심도)
- lens flare (렌즈 플레어)
- motion blur (모션 블러)
- long exposure (장노출)
- double exposure (이중노출)
- matte finish (매트한 마감)

## 예시 프롬프트

### 인물 초상화
```
portrait of a weathered fisherman with deep wrinkles and kind eyes, shot on Hasselblad medium format, 85mm f/2, natural window light, Rembrandt lighting, shallow depth of field, film grain, warm earth tones --ar 3:4 --style raw --stylize 80
```

### 풍경
```
misty mountain landscape at golden hour, layered mountain silhouettes, warm orange and purple sky, shot on 24mm wide angle lens, Fujifilm Velvia colors, sharp focus throughout, dramatic clouds, nature photography --ar 16:9 --style raw --stylize 100
```

### 제품 사진
```
luxury mechanical watch on dark slate surface, studio three-point lighting with soft shadows, sharp focus on watch face details, shallow depth of field, product photography, shot on 100mm macro, premium commercial quality --ar 1:1 --style raw --stylize 50
```

### 시네마틱
```
a lone figure walking through rain-soaked neon-lit Tokyo alley at night, anamorphic lens, cinematic color grading teal and orange, CineStill 800T halation, shallow depth of field, street photography, moody atmospheric --ar 21:9 --style raw --stylize 120
```
