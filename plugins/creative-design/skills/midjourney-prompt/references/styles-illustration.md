## 색상 팔레트 키워드

### 따뜻한 계열
- warm earth tones (어스톤)
- autumn palette, fall colors (가을 색감)
- golden warm palette (따뜻한 골드)
- sunset colors (석양 색감)
- terracotta and amber (테라코타/앰버)

### 차가운 계열
- cool blue tones (차가운 블루)
- winter palette, icy colors (겨울/얼음 색감)
- oceanic blues and greens (바다 색감)
- moonlit silver and blue (달빛 은빛/파랑)

### 파스텔/부드러운
- pastel colors, soft muted palette
- cotton candy palette (솜사탕 파스텔)
- lavender and mint tones
- baby blue and pink

### 선명한/비비드
- vibrant saturated colors
- bold primary colors
- neon bright colors
- jewel tones (보석 톤)

### 제한된 팔레트
- monochromatic (단색 계열)
- limited color palette (제한된 색상)
- duotone (투톤)
- complementary colors (보색)
- analogous colors (유사색)
- split complementary (분할 보색)

## 텍스처/질감 표현

- textured paper, rough paper surface
- canvas texture
- grainy, noisy texture
- smooth gradients
- splatter effects, ink splashes
- visible brushstrokes
