### 스프라이트 공통 키워드
- `isolated white background` 또는 `transparent background`
- `clean pixel edges, no anti-aliasing`
- `consistent art style, cohesive palette`
- `grid layout, organized sheet`

## 2D 격투 게임 특화

### 캐릭터 키워드
- fighting game character design
- martial arts stance, battle ready pose
- dynamic action pose, mid-attack
- muscular/athletic build
- distinctive silhouette
- expressive face, intense expression

### 스타일 참조
- Street Fighter style (카툰 리얼)
- Guilty Gear style (애니메이션 셀셰이드)
- King of Fighters style (세미 리얼 애니)
- Skullgirls style (핸드드로운 애니메이션)

### 이펙트
- energy aura, ki blast, special move effect
- impact frame, hit spark
- speed lines, motion blur
- particle effects, elemental effects
